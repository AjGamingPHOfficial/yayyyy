About Us!

History of our product!

We were planning to make a product that is pastries and baked goods, but we changed it to street foods and changed it again, then we combined our previous idea and we made our finally product that is a cake pop, a pastry and looks like a street food (The Kwek-kwek). The experience of buying a cake pop is expensive to some people and they must go to malls or cafs just to buy cake pops but, here our product will be some sort of substitution for them, so that they can able to eat cake pops and buy them at an affordable price.
